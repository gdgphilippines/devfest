Code Blaze Hackathon

During GDG DevFest Philippines 2016, there will be a hackathon parallel to the event. Below are the mechanics and the form to register

Mechanics

  1. Team must be composed of at most 3 members.
  2. Teams must completely fill out and submit the Application Form. Deadline of application is on September 23, 2016.
  3. Participants should be working professionals.
  4. To qualify, teams should submit existing or build a new application using Google Technologies to showcase their skills. For website, share the link of the app. For Mobile and IoT apps, share the video app demonstration link.
  5. We will announce the top 5 teams who will compete for the hackathon 1 week before DevFest.
  6. Team members will automatically secure their slot to attend DevFest. Wohoo!
  7. Hackathon theme will be announced on the day of the event.
  8. Hackathon mechanics will be emailed to the accepted teams.

Criteria for Judging

Functionality (App Completeness) 30%
Creativity (App Uniqueness) 20%
Usability (UI/UX) 20%
Impact (Overall impact of app to society/ target users) 10%
Presentation 10%
