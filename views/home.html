What is GDG DevFest?

GDG DevFests are large, community-run events that can offer speaker sessions across multiple product areas, all-day hack-a-thons, code labs, and more. In 2016, the official DevFest Season runs from September 01st through November 30th.

GDG DevFest provides the opportunity for developers to learn about Google technologies and other developer products. There are hands-on sessions especially on new technologies.

2015

GDG DevFest Philippines 2015 was held at PLDT Innolab, Mandulayong City on October 16-17, 2015.

On the first day which was focused on Android & VR, there were hands-on workshops on Android 6.0 Marshmallow, Android Basics, Firebase, Material Design, and Google Cardboard.

The second day was focused on Web and a bit of Gemma Kit. Participants joined hands-on workshops on Polymer, Firebase, Material Design Lite, HTML5 Game Development.
